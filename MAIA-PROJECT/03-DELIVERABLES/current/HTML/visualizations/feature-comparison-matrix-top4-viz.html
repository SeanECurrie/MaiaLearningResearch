Maia's international dominance, deep localization, counselor expertise, and comprehensive platform are powerful differentiators. However, Maia faces significant threats from:

  • SCOIR (innovation, AI, pricing)

  • Xello (career-first, pricing, statewide contracts)

  1. Deepen Common App integration
  2. Develop predictive AI (match SCOIR's Scoir AI)
  3. Justify pricing premium OR develop tiered model
  4. Enhance career exploration depth

  7. Double down on international markets (safe zone)
