## Why Top 4 Focus?

### Competitive Rationale

**These 4 competitors represent Maia's primary US competitive threats:**

1. **Naviance** (40% market share) - Legacy leader with dominant market position but poor customer satisfaction (3.2/5)
2. **SCOIR** (12% market share, 40-50% growth) - Innovation leader with AI 2.0, highest satisfaction (4.7/5), greatest threat
3. **SchooLinks** ($88.3M funding) - Value leader ($3.50-5.51/student), state compliance specialist (Texas CCMR)
4. **Xello** (28-year veteran) - Career-first specialist ($3.60/student), statewide contract model

**Why Excluded Competitors Were Filtered Out:**

- **Cialfo:** Asia-Pacific geographic focus (minimal US presence), poor reviews (2.4/5) = different market
- **MajorClarity:** CTE niche focus, US-only, limited brand recognition = different product category
- **Common App:** NOT a competitor - essential infrastructure partner for all platforms
