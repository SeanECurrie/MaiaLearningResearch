## Executive Summary

Maia Learning is a fast-growing, minority-owned company providing a comprehensive cloud-based college planning and career readiness platform for K-12 schools, colleges, and universities globally. Founded in 2010, the company serves over 2.5 million students across 70+ countries and 18,000 schools/universities, with particular strength in international schools and U.S. districts.

**Key Differentiators:**
- Strong international presence (70+ countries)
- Comprehensive K-5 through college platform
- Recently launched AI-powered features
- Minority-owned, customer-focused culture
- Flexible, modern interface requiring minimal training

## Company Overview

### Basic Information
- **Company Name:** MaiaLearning, Inc.
- **Founded:** 2010
- **Headquarters:** Cupertino, California (Previously Palo Alto)
- **CEO/Founder:** Satish Mirle
- **Company Type:** Private, Minority-Owned
- **Employees:** 50+ across 8 countries
- **Website:** https://www.maialearning.com

### Company Mission
"Guiding Students Towards Thriving Futures" - Empowering students, families, and counselors through personalized college and career planning tools.

### Founding Story
CEO Satish Mirle founded Maia Learning in 2010 after experiencing frustrations with existing platforms during his daughter's college application process. He wanted to create a modern, engaging experience with best-in-class service. The company was designed around listening to students, counselors, and schools to meet their actual needs.
