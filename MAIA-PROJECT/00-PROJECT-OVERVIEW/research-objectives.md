# Research Objectives

**Project:** Maia Learning Competitive Analysis  
**Date Established:** 11-16-2024  
**Status:** Active

---

## Primary Objective

Conduct an enterprise-grade competitive analysis of Maia Learning's position in the college and career readiness platform market to inform strategic decision-making and competitive positioning.

---

## Key Research Questions

### Market Position
1. How does Maia Learning compare to market leader Naviance in terms of features, pricing, and market share?
2. What are the key differentiators that set Maia Learning apart from competitors?
3. Which competitors pose the greatest threat and why?
4. What white space opportunities exist in the market?

### Product & Features
5. What features do competitors offer that Maia Learning lacks?
6. Where does Maia Learning have feature superiority?
7. How do AI capabilities compare across platforms?
8. What integrations and partnerships do competitors maintain?

### Market Segments
9. Which customer segments (K-12 public/private, international schools, districts) does each competitor target?
10. How do competitors segment their offerings for different school sizes?
11. What geographic markets are competitors focusing on?

### Business Model
12. What are the pricing structures and models across competitors?
13. How do competitors approach customer acquisition and retention?
14. What is the typical implementation and onboarding process?

### Strategic Trends
15. What major market trends are shaping the industry?
16. How are competitors responding to AI/technology disruption?
17. What M&A activity or consolidation is occurring?
18. How are customer needs evolving?

## Risk Factors & Mitigation

### Data Availability Risk
**Risk:** Private companies may not disclose pricing or detailed product information  
**Mitigation:** Use multiple sources, indirect evidence, customer reviews

### Information Currency Risk
**Risk:** Information becomes outdated quickly in fast-moving market  
**Mitigation:** Focus on 2024-2025 data, note publication dates, plan for updates

### Bias Risk
**Risk:** Company marketing materials may not reflect actual capabilities  
**Mitigation:** Triangulate with customer reviews, third-party analysis

### Scope Creep Risk
**Risk:** Analysis could expand beyond 7 competitors indefinitely  
**Mitigation:** Clear scope definition, parking lot for future research areas

## Iterative Research Framework

This project is designed for **balanced initial coverage** with **focused deep dives** as needed:

1. **Baseline Pass:** Gather foundational information across all dimensions
2. **Gap Identification:** Note areas needing deeper investigation
3. **Priority Deep Dives:** Conduct focused research on high-value areas
4. **Continuous Updates:** Incorporate new information as discovered

**Deep Dive Triggers:**
- Strategic decision requiring more detail
- Conflicting information needing resolution
- High-impact area with insufficient data
- Emerging trend requiring deeper understanding
