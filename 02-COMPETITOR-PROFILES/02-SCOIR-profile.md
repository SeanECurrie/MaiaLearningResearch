## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Company:** SCOIR, Inc.
**Positioning:** Modern, network-based college and career readiness platform challenging Naviance's market dominance
**Market Share:** 12% (as of 2023)
**Growth Rate:** 40-50% annually
**Key Differentiator:** Scoir AI (Admission Intelligence) + Common App Integration + Network Effect ("1 in 8 college-bound students")

SCOIR is a rapidly growing challenger to Naviance, positioning itself as the modern, user-friendly alternative with innovative AI-powered features and strategic integrations (Common App, Coalition, Slate). Founded in 2013 by Irish entrepreneur Gerry McCrory and Kevin McCloskey, SCOIR has built a comprehensive three-sided network connecting students, high schools, and colleges. The company's recent Scoir AI launch (2024-2025) and Common App integration (2025-26) represent significant competitive moves that directly address counselor pain points.

**College Discovery & Research:**

  - College lists sync between Scoir and Common App

  - Enhanced document sending workflow
  - Application-agnostic approach maintained
- Apply Coalition with Scoir
  - Direct application submission to Coalition colleges
  - Integration with Slate CRM
- Multiple application pathway support

**Scoir AI - Admission Intelligence (Flagship Feature):**

Launched September 2024, updated to 2.0 in January 2025. Powered by **tens of millions of de-identified outcome records** and machine learning models.

*Key Components:*

1. **Predictive Chances**
   - Acceptance likelihood predictions by application round (ED, EA, RD)
   - Goes beyond GPA and test scores
   - Considers: first-gen status, geography, race/ethnicity, sex, high school profile
   - Shows personalized acceptance predictions for each college
   - Multiple touchpoints throughout student journey

2. **Balanced List Scores**
   - Evaluates overall health of college list
   - Generates "College List Score" (grade A-F)
   - Helps ensure lists aren't too reach-heavy or safety-heavy
   - Tracks list balance over time

3. **Intelligent Match**
   - Automatically assigns acceptance likelihood labels (reach/target/safety)
   - Works at scale for entire student population
   - Saves counselors manual research time
   - Customizable match criteria

**Other Features:**
- Scattergrams (historical acceptance data from student's high school)
- Mobile app (iOS)
- Help desk and email support
- Scoir U training and certification
- District-level solutions available

## 4. TECHNOLOGY & INNOVATION

### 4.1 Scoir AI - Admission Intelligence (Flagship Innovation)

**Launch Timeline:**
- **September 2024:** Admission Intelligence announced
- **January 2025:** Scoir AI 2.0 released with enhanced features
- **2025 onwards:** Continuous improvements and data refinements

**Technology Foundation:**

**Data Source:**
- **Tens of millions** of de-identified application outcome records
- Real, validated students across the Scoir network
- Historical outcomes from multiple application cycles
- Continuously updated and refined

**Machine Learning Models:**
- Go beyond simple GPA and test score comparisons
- Multidimensional modeling approach

**Factors Considered:**
- Academic: GPA, test scores (when available)
- Demographics: First-generation status, race/ethnicity, sex
- Geographic: Student location, in-state vs out-of-state trends
- Institutional: High school profile and historical outcomes
- Application timing: ED, EA, RD rounds

**Privacy & Security:**
- All data is de-identified (strips PII)
- Student privacy fully protected
- Complies with data protection regulations

**Predictive Intelligence Components:**

1. **Predictive Chances**
   - Individual acceptance likelihood predictions
   - Round-specific predictions (ED vs EA vs RD)
   - Percentage-based predictions (e.g., "68% chance")
   - Visible to students and counselors
   - Multiple touchpoints throughout application process
   - Helps students understand realistic acceptance chances

2. **Balanced List Scores**
   - Evaluates overall college list composition
   - Generates letter grade (A+ to F)
   - Ensures appropriate mix of reach/target/safety schools
   - Helps counselors quickly assess list health
   - Gamification element motivates students to balance lists

3. **Intelligent Match**
   - Automated acceptance likelihood labeling
   - Works at scale for entire student body
   - Customizable by counselors (can adjust parameters)
   - Saves counselors hours of manual research
   - Auto-updates as student profile changes

**Scoir AI Smart Features:**
- Smarter class year selection (uses most relevant historical data)
- Location-aware predictions (in-state vs out-of-state)
- Scattergram enhancements
- Predictive insights for underclassmen
- School-specific customization

**Counselor Controls:**
- Full control over when students see predictions
- Ability to enable/disable features by grade level
- Customizable balanced list requirements
- Opt-in/opt-out flexibility
- Settings for predictive chances visibility
- Separate controls for scattergrams

**Industry Reception:**

- Addresses counselor concern about misinformation from chance calculators

### 4.2 Common App Integration (Game-Changing Feature)

**Launch Timeline:**
- **March 2025:** Integration announced
- **2025-26 Academic Year:** Live for schools that opted in
- **Most requested feature** from counselors

**How It Works:**

1. **College Lists In-Sync**
   - Students' "My Colleges" lists in Scoir sync with Common App activity
   - Counselors see where students applied and when
   - Automatic updates when students add/submit applications
   - Single source of truth for college list

2. **No More Duplicate Recommendation Requests**
   - Common App recognizes "Scoir schools"
   - Does NOT prompt students to request recommendations in Common App
   - All recommendation requests go through Scoir
   - Eliminates counselor/teacher confusion

3. **Application Flexibility**
   - Scoir remains application-agnostic
   - Students can use Common App, Apply Coalition with Scoir, or other methods
   - Flexibility to apply however best serves student

4. **Enhanced Document Sending**
   - Improved overall document sending experience
   - Works across all application methods
   - Streamlined workflow for counselors

**Implementation Requirements:**
- Schools must opt-in to enable integration
- Configure School Report Template in Scoir
- Assign counselors to seniors
- Complete required setup steps

**School Control:**
- Each school decides whether to enable integration
- No forced adoption
- Can enable/disable by academic year
- Counselor-controlled settings

**Competitive Impact:**
- Eliminates major pain point (dual systems management)
- Reduces counselor workload significantly
- Increases Scoir stickiness (switching costs rise)
- Neutralizes Naviance's Common App integration advantage
- Major differentiator vs Maia Learning (no Common App integration)
