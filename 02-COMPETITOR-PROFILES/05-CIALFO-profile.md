## 1. Company Background

### Founding Story & Evolution

**Early Years (2010-2016):**
- **2010:** Co-founders Rohan Pasari and Stanley Chia meet at Nanyang Technological University's Minor in Entrepreneurship program in Singapore
- **2012:** Start Cialfo as **offline consultancy** for higher education counseling
- **2012:** First success - helped student secure admission to Wharton (University of Pennsylvania)
- **2012-2016:** Built reputation as boutique college admissions consultancy
- **2016:** Validation of platform idea with seed round led by Govin Capital and Koh Boon Hwee
- **2016:** William Hund joins as third co-founder and Head of Product
- **2016:** Opens first international office in Shanghai, China

**Pivot to EdTech Platform (2017):**
- **2017:** Co-founders sell consultancy business to focus on technology solutions at scale

## 3. Pricing & Business Model

### Pricing Structure

**Per-Student Pricing Model:**
- **Average pricing: $30 per student** (according to January Capital investment analysis, 2020)
- Pricing varies by:
  - School size (volume discounts)
  - Geographic region
  - Features included
  - Contract length (multi-year discounts)
  - Bundle with additional services (Cialfo Guides, etc.)

**Pricing Examples:**
- Some sources mention **$39/month flat rate** (Capterra listing)
- Comparable to competitors (SchooLinks $3.50-5.51/student, Xello $3.60/student, Scoir $4.80/student)
- **Significantly cheaper than Maia** ($30 vs. Maia's $7-9/student is INCORRECT - $30 is HIGHER than Maia)

**Note:** Pricing is NOT publicly transparent - quote-based model

### Business Model

**B2B2C Model:**
- **Primary customer:** K12 schools and districts (B2B)
- **End users:** Students, counselors, parents (B2C via schools)
- Schools pay subscription, students/families use free
- Premium services (Cialfo Guides) may have additional costs

**Subscription-Based Revenue:**
- Annual subscriptions with recurring revenue
- Multi-year contracts (3-5 years typical)
- Predictable revenue stream
- High renewal rates due to switching costs

**Three Revenue Streams:**

1. **K12 Platform Subscriptions:**
   - Schools pay per-student annual fee
   - Core revenue stream
   - Includes platform access, integrations, support

2. **University Partnerships (Explore):**
   - Universities pay to access Cialfo's student network
   - International High School Tour fees
   - Recruitment marketing services
   - Lead generation fees
   - Creates two-sided marketplace economics

3. **Premium Services:**
   - Cialfo Guides (expert counseling)
   - Counseling Excellence Lab (professional development)
   - Custom integrations or implementations
   - White-label or branded solutions potential

**Freemium Element:**
- **2020:** Offered free version during COVID-19 pandemic
- Helped students apply to college during school closures
- May still have limited free tier for individual students (unclear)
